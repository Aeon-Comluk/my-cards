"You Are Loved"
by Aeon

As expected,
you want something different.
That’s one of the main reasons
why you are a part of my system.
I love the way you think.
I love the way you listen.
On things that others stumble over,
you understand the mission.

As we get older,
and life keeps updating its status,
we all need to know
we have someone in our corner
that has us.
It’s a comfort that doesn’t come quickly,
but once it comes,
it’s so strong.

I hope our journey to it has started,
seeing that we have known each other so long.
I admire the things you aspire to,
even though you don’t have it all figured out.
I want you to see your true worth,
beyond what comes out of your mouth.

You're at a place of endless potential now.
And the way forward might be unclear.
As you try to figure out how
to make it to a goal
that is so vague
and not very near.

But I know you can make it,
even if you are not sure that you can.
Because I’ve been there too.
Trust me, I understand.

The trick is not to try to fit into the box,
but to trust yourself enough
to do it your way
and relax.

There is no correct way
to do things in this life —
just the way that works, the way that is wise.

I hope these words resonate with you
and calm you on this day.
And for what it’s worth,
I just want to say, hey.

The next time you see me
and I’m behaving all tough,
it’s all just a front.
I care about you…
You are loved.
